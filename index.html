Our Mission Statement

To build a powerful, online community for young entrepreneurs to learn more about building companies from VCs, CEOs, and each other.

How it Works

OnePager.io brings together the top 21 most creative, impressive students around the country. We hope to lower barriers to entry for top students looking to work on interesting projects, now and in the future.

About Us


We are three students at Harvard extremely passionate about startups and entrepreneurship. We each have a variety of interests and experiences, from design, fintech, recruiting, working on side projects, and even seed stage venture capital through Dorm Room Fund and Rough Draft Ventures. We wish this type of community existed for our friends around us equally passionate about building things, and so decided to create this community.
