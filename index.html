How it Works

OnePager.io brings together the top 21 most creative, impressive students around the country and connects them with each other, top-tier VCs, and resources to help them build.

Meet other Talented Students

Connect with a powerful community of students from Harvard, Stanford, MIT, and other schools around the country.

Meet Founders and VCs

This group gets exclusive access to partners at elite VC firms and founders from successful startups through bi-weekly conference calls.

Get Access to Credits + Resources

Thousands of Dollars worth of hosting, legal, and other credits to help you build. Github, AWS, and the works.

About Us


OnePager.io started as a project for three friends at Harvard. Collectively, we’ve spent time working as engineers, designers, founders, and even investors through Dorm Room Fund. Over the summer, after a year of looking for a community like OnePager.io elsewhere, we decided to build it ourselves.

We're actively looking to grow the team! Please get in touch if you'd like to give this community direction.
